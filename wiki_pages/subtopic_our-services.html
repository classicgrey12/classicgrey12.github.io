Welcome to CoDEHub

Revolutionizing data management for Mercedes-Benz

The Smart Data Layer (SDL) is a cutting-edge data management solution designed specifically for Mercedes-Benz. It provides a unified platform for data integration, analysis, and governance across all departments and operations.

By leveraging advanced technologies and best practices in data management, SDL empowers Mercedes-Benz to make data-driven decisions, optimize processes, and deliver exceptional value to our customers.

Prerequisites for your work

SDL's mission is to provide a unified, secure, and efficient data management solution for Mercedes-Benz, enabling data-driven decision making across all levels of the organization.

  • Empower teams with real-time access to critical data
  • Ensure data integrity and consistency across the organization
  • Facilitate collaboration and knowledge sharing
  • Drive innovation through advanced analytics capabilities

  • Streamlined data governance and compliance management
